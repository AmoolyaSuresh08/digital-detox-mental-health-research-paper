# Digital Detox: The Impact of Technology Overload on Mental Health

## About the Paper
This repository contains my research paper on the impact of excessive technology use on mental health. The paper explores how prolonged screen time and digital overload contribute to stress, anxiety, and reduced cognitive performance, and examines digital detox as a strategy to improve well-being.

## What the Paper Covers
- Effects of technology overload on mental health  
- Relationship between screen time and stress, anxiety, and productivity  
- Benefits and importance of digital detox  
- Strategies for healthier and balanced technology use  

## Purpose
This research was conducted to analyze the psychological effects of technology overuse and to highlight solutions for maintaining better mental and cognitive health.
